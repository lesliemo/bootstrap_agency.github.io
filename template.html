  • 2008-2015

    Humble Beginnings

    In exchange for my 4-year full tuition scholarship, I worked in integrated marketing & project administration to attract donors and prospective students to Berea College. After becoming an award receipient for several labor awards, and the Peterson Spring Award for applied technology, I got hired to work as full time staff member responsible for building and coordinating the information management systems for both the rec center and the athletic department.

  • August 2015

    A High Tech Professional is Born

    I had acquired enough IT and technical experience to be trusted to manage the workflows of 7 web developers, 3 graphic designers, and 2 videographers... but to be fair the group was incredibly talented! My role was mostly to acquire clients and translate their needs into project requirements, then manage the budget and project lifecycle. In the meantime, I assisted with event marketing and economic developement programming centered around LEAN & agile entrepreneurship (e.g. 5across pitch competitions, Startup Weekend, Global Entrepreneurship Week).

  • December 2012

    Meet the Maker

    But not to fear, I did not let my other skill sets atrophy! I used my digital marketing expertise to assist other community groups including a tenure helping my local makerspace in their efforts to scale!

  • July 2014

    Digital Inclusion to-scale

    Had an unexpected career pivot when my instructional media position was eliminated after I relocated to Louisville! I still managed about 40 client accounts for smaller tiered higher ed institutions, but found that I really missed the design & particpatory facilitation that came from working with peers to develop scalable solutions. Now I'm back in the fray!
